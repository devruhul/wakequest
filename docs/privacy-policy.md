# WakeQuest Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 8, 2026

WakeQuest is a smart alarm clock app that helps users wake up by completing
missions such as maths questions, memory codes, walking, and experimental
on-device push-up detection.

## Data WakeQuest stores

WakeQuest stores alarm settings, mission settings, wake streaks, and wake
statistics locally on your device. This includes alarm times, labels, repeat
days, mission type, memory mission settings, step goals, and completion
statistics.

WakeQuest does not currently create an online account, upload alarm data, or
sync user data to a cloud service.

## Camera use

WakeQuest uses the camera for experimental push-up pose detection. Camera frames
are processed on the device for the selected mission. WakeQuest does not upload
camera images or videos to a server.

## Activity recognition and steps

WakeQuest may request activity-recognition permission to count steps for walking
missions. Step-count information is used to complete the current mission and is
not uploaded to a server.

## Notifications and alarms

WakeQuest uses notification, exact-alarm, full-screen intent, vibration, wake
lock, and boot-completed permissions so alarms can ring reliably, wake the
screen, and continue working after device restart.

## Microphone and location

The current version of WakeQuest does not use the microphone or location. Future
versions may add voice missions or location-based features only after requesting
the relevant permission and updating this policy.

## Third-party services

WakeQuest uses Flutter packages and on-device Google ML Kit pose detection for
some functionality. The current app does not send personal alarm data to a
backend service.

## Data deletion

You can delete alarms inside the app. You can also remove all locally stored app
data by uninstalling WakeQuest or clearing the app storage from Android system
settings.

## Children

WakeQuest is not designed specifically for children.

## Changes to this policy

This policy may be updated when WakeQuest adds new features such as accounts,
cloud backup, subscriptions, or additional AI missions.
