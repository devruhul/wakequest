WakeQuest Privacy Policy

Effective date: 11 July 2026

WakeQuest ("the app", "we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information the app may process, why it is used, and the choices available to you.

Information processed by the app

WakeQuest may process the following information to provide its features:

  • Alarm times, labels, repeat schedules, mission choices, and app preferences.
  • Notification permission so the app can alert you when an alarm is due.
  • Activity recognition data, such as step or movement information, when you use a movement-based wake-up mission.
  • Camera access when you choose a camera-based mission. Camera access is only used while that feature is active.
  • Basic technical information needed for the app to function correctly, such as app version and device operating-system version.

How information is used

Information is used only to provide and improve WakeQuest features, including scheduling alarms, saving app settings, showing notifications, and verifying wake-up missions selected by the user.

Local storage

Alarm settings and preferences may be stored locally on your device. Removing the app may remove locally stored app data, depending on your device settings.

Data sharing and sale

WakeQuest does not sell or rent personal information. WakeQuest does not share personal information with third parties for advertising purposes.

Permissions

The app may request permissions such as notifications, activity recognition, camera, vibration, wake lock, and exact alarms. Permissions are requested only when needed for app functionality. You can review or disable permissions in your device settings, although some features may then stop working.

Data security

We use reasonable measures to protect information handled by the app. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure.

Data retention and deletion

Locally stored alarms and preferences remain on your device until you delete them, clear the app's storage, or uninstall the app. If cloud-based features are introduced in the future, this policy will be updated before those features are released.

Children

WakeQuest is intended for users aged 18 and over and is not directed to children.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy when the app changes. The effective date at the top of this page will be updated when revisions are made.
